About us

At True Platform, we focus on the most competitive part (and probably most exciting) of enterprises: customer-based applications. We want to build the easiest-to-start, simplest-to-use, and fastest-time-to-value customer-centric platform that help companies win deals, grow loyalties, communicate seamlessly and work better with customers.

What we are looking from you

Solid background knowledge

A solid foundation of your major helps you succeed anywhere. If you are a Software Engineer, it's great that you have BS or higher in Information Technology, Computer Science, Mathematics or any related field. Make sure you hold the key to success of any high performance and scalable product: Data Structures & Algorithms, Programming Language.

Product mindset & Problem solving

Building products with True Platform, you can play with ideas and iterations daily to solve the hardest problems. Always ask “why” to make sure you deliver real values to real customers.

Ambition to take challenges

Keep dreaming big to build a global product company from first steps! Being earliest members comes with a great deal of challenges, but we believe your willingness to learn can drive you to be a part of something really big, really innovative, and really impactful.

"The best or nothing" spirit

It wasn't easy to accept the fact that if we couldn't work hard enough, we could never make anything special. Here we want to build a special company, and we need that spirit from you. Try your best, and create extraordinary outcomes.

Why you should join us

Fast-track career path

Enhance your expertise through a comprehensive training program, constant 1-1 guidelines from leaders, senior members, and great opportunities to join a full cycle of building real products for the global market.

Talented colleagues

Benefits package
