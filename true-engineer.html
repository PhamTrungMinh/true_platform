True Engineer is a talent program for young Software Engineers (Fresher/Junior) who desire to build valuable and high-performance products for the global market. As a Software Engineer at True Platform & Base.vn, with a comprehensive training program and guidelines from talented Engineers, you can gain the necessary skills to build a complete product and join in the official product development process after 01 month.

True Platform and Base.vn provide technology management solutions for SMEs with a mission to build great enterprise software (B2B SaaS) for the world. After 6 years of establishment, Base.vn leads the Vietnamese market with 7000+ corporate customers and hundreds of thousands users each month.

Continuing the mission of Base.vn, True Platform focuses on the global market with an easiest-to-start, simplest-to-use, and fastest-time-to-value customer-centric platform that helps companies win deals, grow loyalties, communicate seamlessly, and work better with customers.

Who is our True Engineer

Final-year students or graduates with major in IT and related fields (≤ 2 years of work experience, can work full-time)
Solid background knowledge in Data Structures & Algorithms
Strong knowledge or experience in using any programming language (prefer PHP/Javascript/ React Native)
Passion for coding and strong desire to build valuable products
Logical thinking and good problem-solving skills
Willingness to learn, dream big and dare to address challenges
